<p><a href = "#chapter/book4--chapter1.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Numbers 2</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book4--chapter3.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And تتراجراماتون spoke unto Musa and unto Haron (Aaron), saying,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> The children of Israel shall encamp every man by his own standard, with the ensigns of their fathers’ houses: over against the tent of meeting shall they encamp round about.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> And those that encamp on the east side toward the sunrising shall be they of the standard of the camp of Judah, according to their hosts: and the prince of the children of Judah shall be Nahshon the son of Amminadab.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were threescore and fourteen thousand and six hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> And those that encamp next unto him shall be the tribe of Issachar: and the prince of the children of Issachar shall be Nethanel the son of Zuar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, were fifty and four thousand and four hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And the tribe of Zebulun: and the prince of the children of Zebulun shall be Eliab the son of Helon.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, were fifty and seven thousand and four hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> All that were numbered of the camp of Judah were a hundred thousand and fourscore thousand and six thousand and four hundred, according to their hosts.
<br>
 They shall set forth first.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> On the south side shall be the standard of the camp of Reuben according to their hosts: and the prince of the children of Reuben shall be Elizur the son of Shedeur.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered thereof, were forty and six thousand and five hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> And those that encamp next unto him shall be the tribe of Simeon: and the prince of the children of Simeon shall be Shelumiel the son of Zurishaddai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were fifty and nine thousand and three hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> And the tribe of Gad: and the prince of the children of Gad shall be Eliasaph the son of Reuel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were forty and five thousand and six hundred and fifty.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> All that were numbered of the camp of Reuben were a hundred thousand and fifty and one thousand and four hundred and fifty, according to their hosts.
<br>
 And they shall set forth second.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> Then the tent of meeting shall set forward, with the camp of the Levites in the midst of the camps: as they encamp, so shall they set forward, every man in his place, by their standards.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> On the west side shall be the standard of the camp of Ephraim according to their hosts: and the prince of the children of Ephraim shall be Elishama the son of Ammihud.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were forty thousand and five hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> And next unto him shall be the tribe of Manasseh: and the prince of the children of Manasseh shall be Gamaliel the son of Pedahzur.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were thirty and two thousand and two hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> And the tribe of Benjamin: and the prince of the children of Benjamin shall be Abidan the son of Gideoni.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were thirty and five thousand and four hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> All that were numbered of the camp of Ephraim were a hundred thousand and eight thousand and a hundred, according to their hosts.
<br>
 And they shall set forth third.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> On the north side shall be the standard of the camp of Dan according to their hosts: and the prince of the children of Dan shall be Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were threescore and two thousand and seven hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> And those that encamp next unto him shall be the tribe of Asher: and the prince of the children of Asher shall be Pagiel the son of Ochran.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> And his hosts, and those that were numbered of them, were forty and one thousand and five hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> And the tribe of Naphtali: and the prince of the children of Naphtali shall be Ahira the son of Enan.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> And his host, and those that were numbered of them, were fifty and three thousand and four hundred.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> All that were numbered of the camp of Dan were a hundred thousand and fifty and seven thousand and six hundred.
<br>
 They shall set forth hindmost by their standards.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> These are they that were numbered of the children of Israel by their fathers’ houses: all that were numbered of the camps according to their hosts were six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">33</sup> But the Levites were not numbered among the children of Israel; as تتراجراماتون commanded Musa.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> Thus did the children of Israel; according to all that تتراجراماتون commanded Musa, so they encamped by their standards, and so they set forward, every one by their families, according to their fathers’ houses.</sup>
<br>
