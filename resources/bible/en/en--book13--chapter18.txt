<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And after this it came to pass, that David (Dawad) smote the Philistines, and subdued them, and took Gath and its towns out of the hand of the Philistines.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> And he smote Moab; and the Moabites became servants to David (Dawad), and brought tribute.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> And David (Dawad) smote Hadarezer king of Zobah unto Hamath, as he went to establish his dominion by the river Euphrates.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And David (Dawad) took from him a thousand chariots, and seven thousand horsemen, and twenty thousand footmen; and David (Dawad) hocked all the chariot horses, but reserved of them for a hundred chariots.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> And when the Syrians of Damascus came to succor Hadarezer king of Zobah, David (Dawad) smote of the Syrians two and twenty thousand men.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Then David (Dawad) put garrisons in Syria of Damascus; and the Syrians became servants to David (Dawad), and brought tribute.
<br>
 And تتراجراماتون gave victory to David (Dawad) whithersoever he went.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And David (Dawad) took the shields of gold that were on the servants of Hadarezer, and brought them to Jerusalem.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And from Tibhath and from Cun, cities of Hadarezer, David (Dawad) took very much brass, wherewith Solomon made the brazen sea, and the pillars, and the vessels of brass.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> And when Tou king of Hamath heard that David (Dawad) had smitten all the host of Hadarezer king of Zobah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> he sent Hadoram his son to king David (Dawad), to salute him, and to bless him, because he had fought against Hadarezer and smitten him (for Hadarezer had wars with Tou); and he had with him all manner of vessels of gold and silver and brass.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> These also did king David (Dawad) dedicate unto تتراجراماتون, with the silver and the gold that he carried away from all the nations; from Edom, and from Moab, and from the children of Ammon, and from the Philistines, and from Amalek.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> Moreover Abishai the son of Zeruiah smote of the Edomites in the Valley of Salt eighteen thousand.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> And he put garrisons in Edom; and all the Edomites became servants to David (Dawad).
<br>
 And تتراجراماتون gave victory to David (Dawad) whithersoever he went.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> And David (Dawad) reigned over all Israel; and he executed justice and righteousness unto all his people.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> And Joab the son of Zeruiah was over the host; and Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud was recorder; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> and Zadok the son of Ahitub, and Abimelech the son of Abiathar, were priests; and Shavsha was scribe; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the Cherethites and the Pelethites; and the sons of David (Dawad) were chief about the king.
<br>
<br>
</div>
