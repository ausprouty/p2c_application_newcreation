<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(A Psalm of Dawad, when he fled from Absalom his son.) تتراجراماتون, how are mine adversaries increased!
<br>
Many are they that rise up against me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Many there are that say of my soul,
<br>
There is no help for him in Elokim. Selah
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> But thou, O تتراجراماتون, art a shield about me;
<br>
My glory, and the lifter up of my head.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> I cry unto تتراجراماتون with my voice,
<br>
And he answereth me out of his holy hill.
<br>
Selah
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> I laid me down and slept;
<br>
I awaked; for تتراجراماتون sustaineth me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> I will not be afraid of ten thousands of the people
<br>
That have set themselves against me round about.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Arise, O تتراجراماتون; save me, O my Elokim:
<br>
For thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the cheek bone;
<br>
Thou hast broken the teeth of the wicked.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> Salvation belongeth unto تتراجراماتون:
<br>
Thy blessing be upon thy people.
<br>
Selah
<br>
<br>
</div>
