<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter147.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
Praise تتراجراماتون, O my soul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> While I live will I praise تتراجراماتون:
<br>
I will sing praises unto my Elokim while I have any being.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Put not your trust in princes,
<br>
Nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth;
<br>
In that very day his thoughts perish.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Happy is he that hath the Elokim of Ya’qub (Jacob) for his help,
<br>
Whose hope is in تتراجراماتون his Elokim:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Who made heaven and earth,
<br>
The sea, and all that in them is;
<br>
Who keepeth truth for ever;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Who executeth justice for the oppressed;
<br>
Who giveth food to the hungry.
<br>
تتراجراماتون looseth the prisoners;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> تتراجراماتون openeth the eyes of the blind;
<br>
تتراجراماتون raiseth up them that are bowed down;
<br>
تتراجراماتون loveth the righteous;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> تتراجراماتون preserveth the sojourners;
<br>
He upholdeth the fatherless and widow;
<br>
But the way of the wicked he turneth upside down.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> تتراجراماتون will reign for ever,
<br>
Thy Elokim, O Zion, unto all generations.
<br>
Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<br>
</div>
