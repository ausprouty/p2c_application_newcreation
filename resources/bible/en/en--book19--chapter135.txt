<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter134.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 135</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter136.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
Praise ye the name of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
Praise him, O ye servants of تتراجراماتون,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Ye that stand in the house of تتراجراماتون,
<br>
In the courts of the house of our Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون; for تتراجراماتون is good:
<br>
Sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> For تتراجراماتون hath chosen Ya’qub (Jacob) unto himself,
<br>
And Israel for his own possession.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> For I know that تتراجراماتون is great,
<br>
And that our Lord is above all Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Whatsoever تتراجراماتون pleased, that hath he done,
<br>
In heaven and in earth, in the seas and in all deeps;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Who causeth the vapors to ascend from the ends of the earth;
<br>
Who maketh lightnings for the rain;
<br>
Who bringeth forth the wind out of his treasuries;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> Who smote the first-born of Egypt,
<br>
Both of man and beast;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> Who sent signs and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt,
<br>
Upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> Who smote many nations,
<br>
And slew mighty kings,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Sihon king of the Amorites,
<br>
And Og king of Bashan,
<br>
And all the kingdoms of Canaan,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> And gave their land for a heritage,
<br>
A heritage unto Israel his people.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> Thy name, O تتراجراماتون, endureth for ever;
<br>
Thy memorial name, O تتراجراماتون, throughout all generations.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> For تتراجراماتون will judge his people,
<br>
And repent himself concerning his servants.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> The idols of the nations are silver and gold,
<br>
The work of men’s hands.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> They have mouths, but they speak not;
<br>
Eyes have they, but they see not;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> They have ears, but they hear not;
<br>
Neither is there any breath in their mouths.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> They that make them shall be like unto them;
<br>
Yea, every one that trusteth in them.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> O house of Israel, bless ye تتراجراماتون:
<br>
O house of Haron (Aaron), bless ye تتراجراماتون:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> O house of Levi, bless ye تتراجراماتون:
<br>
Ye that fear تتراجراماتون, bless ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> Blessed be تتراجراماتون out of Zion, Who dwelleth at Jerusalem. Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<br>
</div>
