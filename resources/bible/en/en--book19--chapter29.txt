<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter30.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(A Psalm of Dawad) Ascribe unto تتراجراماتون, O ye sons of the mighty,
<br>
Ascribe unto تتراجراماتون glory and strength.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Ascribe unto تتراجراماتون the glory due unto his name;
<br>
Worship تتراجراماتون in holy array.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون is upon the waters:
<br>
The Elokim of glory thundereth,
<br>
Even تتراجراماتون upon many waters.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون is powerful;
<br>
The voice of تتراجراماتون is full of majesty.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون breaketh the cedars;
<br>
Yea, تتراجراماتون breaketh in pieces the cedars of Lebanon.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> He maketh them also to skip like a calf;
<br>
Lebanon and Sirion like a young wild-ox.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون cleaveth the flames of fire.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون shaketh the wilderness;
<br>
تتراجراماتون shaketh the wilderness of Kadesh.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> The voice of تتراجراماتون maketh the hinds to calve,
<br>
And strippeth the forests bare:
<br>
And in his temple everything saith, Glory.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> تتراجراماتون sat as King at the Flood;
<br>
Yea, تتراجراماتون sitteth as King for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> تتراجراماتون will give strength unto his people;
<br>
تتراجراماتون will bless his people with peace.
<br>
<br>
</div>
