<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter112.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
I will give thanks unto تتراجراماتون with my whole heart,
<br>
In the council of the upright, and in the congregation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> The works of تتراجراماتون are great,
<br>
Sought out of all them that have pleasure therein.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> His work is honor and majesty;
<br>
And his righteousness endureth for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> He hath made his wonderful works to be remembered:
<br>
تتراجراماتون is gracious and merciful.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> He hath given food unto them that fear him:
<br>
He will ever be mindful of his covenant.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> He hath showed his people the power of his works,
<br>
In giving them the heritage of the nations.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> The works of his hands are truth and justice;
<br>
All his precepts are sure.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> They are established for ever and ever;
<br>
They are done in truth and uprightness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> He hath sent redemption unto his people;
<br>
He hath commanded his covenant for ever:
<br>
Holy and reverend is his name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> The fear of تتراجراماتون is the beginning of wisdom;
<br>
A good understanding have all they that do his commandments:
<br>
His praise endureth for ever.
<br>
<br>
</div>
