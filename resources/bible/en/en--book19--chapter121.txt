<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Song of Ascents) I will lift up mine eyes unto the mountains:
<br>
From whence shall my help come?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> My help cometh from تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Who made heaven and earth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> He will not suffer thy foot to be moved:
<br>
He that keepeth thee will not slumber.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Behold, he that keepeth Israel
<br>
Will neither slumber nor sleep.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> تتراجراماتون is thy keeper:
<br>
تتراجراماتون is thy shade upon thy right hand.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> The sun shall not smite thee by day,
<br>
Nor the moon by night.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> تتراجراماتون will keep thee from all evil;
<br>
He will keep thy soul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> تتراجراماتون will keep thy going out and thy coming in
<br>
From this time forth and for evermore.
<br>
<br>
</div>
