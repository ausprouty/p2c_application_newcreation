<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter123.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Song of Ascents; of Dawad) I was glad when they said unto me,
<br>
Let us go unto the house of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Our feet are standing
<br>
Within thy gates, O Jerusalem,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Jerusalem, that art builded
<br>
As a city that is compact together;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Whither the tribes go up, even the tribes of تتراجراماتون,
<br>
For an ordinance for Israel,
<br>
To give thanks unto the name of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> For there are set thrones for judgment,
<br>
The thrones of the house of David (Dawad).
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Pray for the peace of Jerusalem:
<br>
They shall prosper that love thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Peace be within thy walls,
<br>
And prosperity within thy palaces.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> For my brethren and companions’ sakes,
<br>
I will now say, Peace be within thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> For the sake of the house of تتراجراماتون our Elokim
<br>
I will seek thy good.
<br>
<br>
</div>
