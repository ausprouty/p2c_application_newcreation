<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Moreover David (Dawad) and the captains of the host set apart for the service certain of the sons of Asaph, and of Heman, and of Jeduthun, who should prophesy with harps, with psalteries, and with cymbals: and the number of them that did the work according to their service was:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> of the sons of Asaph: Zaccur, and Yusuf (Joseph), and Nethaniah, and Asharelah, the sons of Asaph, under the hand of Asaph, who prophesied after the order of the king.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Of Jeduthun; the sons of Jeduthun: Gedaliah, and Zeri, and Jeshaiah, Hashabiah, and Mattithiah, six, under the hands of their father Jeduthun with the harp, who prophesied in giving thanks and praising تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Of Heman; the sons of Heman: Bukkiah, Mattaniah, Uzziel, Shebuel, and Jerimoth, Hananiah, Hanani, Eliathah, Giddalti, and Romamti-ezer, Joshbekashah, Mallothi, Hothir, Mahazioth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> All these were the sons of Heman the king’s seer in the words of Elokim, to lift up the horn.
<br>
 And Elokim gave to Heman fourteen sons and three daughters.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> All these were under the hands of their father for song in the house of تتراجراماتون, with cymbals, psalteries, and harps, for the service of the house of Elokim; Asaph, Jeduthun, and Heman being under the order of the king.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And the number of them, with their brethren that were instructed in singing unto تتراجراماتون, even all that were skilful, was two hundred fourscore and eight.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And they cast lots for their offices, all alike, as well the small as the great, the teacher as the scholar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> Now the first lot came forth for Asaph to Yusuf (Joseph): the second to Gedaliah; he and his brethren and sons were twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> the third to Zaccur, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> the fourth to Izri, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> the fifth to Nethaniah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> the sixth to Bukkiah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> the seventh to Jesharelah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> the eighth to Jeshaiah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> the ninth to Mattaniah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> the tenth to Shimei, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> the eleventh to Azarel, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> the twelfth to Hashabiah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> for the thirteenth, Shubael, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> for the fourteenth, Mattithiah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> for the fifteenth to Jeremoth, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> for the sixteenth to Hananiah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> for the seventeenth to Joshbekashah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> for the eighteenth to Hanani, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> for the nineteenth to Mallothi, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> for the twentieth to Eliathah, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> for the one and twentieth to Hothir, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> for the two and twentieth to Giddalti, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> for the three and twentieth to Mahazioth, his sons and his brethren, twelve: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> for the four and twentieth to Romamtiezer, his sons and his brethren, twelve.
<br>
<br>
</div>
