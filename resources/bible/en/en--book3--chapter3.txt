<p><a href = "#chapter/book3--chapter2.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Leviticus 3</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book3--chapter4.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And if his oblation be a sacrifice of peace-offerings; if he offer of the herd, whether male or female, he shall offer it without blemish before تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his oblation, and kill it at the door of the tent of meeting: and Haron (Aaron)’s sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of peace-offerings an offering made by fire unto تتراجراماتون; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> and the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the loins, and the caul upon the liver, with the kidneys, shall he take away.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> And Haron (Aaron)’s sons shall burn it on the altar upon the burnt-offering, which is upon the wood that is on the fire: it is an offering made by fire, of a sweet savor unto تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And if his oblation for a sacrifice of peace-offerings unto تتراجراماتون be of the flock; male or female, he shall offer it without blemish.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> If he offer a lamb for his oblation, then shall he offer it before تتراجراماتون; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> and he shall lay his hand upon the head of his oblation, and kill it before the tent of meeting: and Haron (Aaron)’s sons shall sprinkle the blood thereof upon the altar round about.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of peace-offerings an offering made by fire unto تتراجراماتون; the fat thereof, the fat tail entire, he shall take away hard by the backbone; and the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> and the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the loins, and the caul upon the liver, with the kidneys, shall he take away.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> And the priest shall burn it upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire unto تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> And if his oblation be a goat, then he shall offer it before تتراجراماتون: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> and he shall lay his hand upon the head of it, and kill it before the tent of meeting; and the sons of Haron (Aaron) shall sprinkle the blood thereof upon the altar round about.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> And he shall offer thereof his oblation, even an offering made by fire unto تتراجراماتون; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> and the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the loins, and the caul upon the liver, with the kidneys, shall he take away.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> And the priest shall burn them upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire, for a sweet savor; all the fat is تتراجراماتون’s.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> It shall be a perpetual statute throughout your generations in all your dwellings, that ye shall eat neither fat nor blood.
<br>
