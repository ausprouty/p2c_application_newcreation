<p><a href = "#chapter/book4--chapter32.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Numbers 33</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book4--chapter34.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> These are the journeys of the children of Israel, when they went forth out of the land of Egypt by their hosts under the hand of Musa and Haron (Aaron).
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> And Musa wrote their goings out according to their journeys by the commandment of تتراجراماتون: and these are their journeys according to their goings out.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> And they journeyed from Rameses in the first month, on the fifteenth day of the first month; on the morrow after the passover the children of Israel went out with a high hand in the sight of all the Egyptians, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> while the Egyptians were burying all their first-born, whom تتراجراماتون had smitten among them: upon their Elokim also تتراجراماتون executed judgments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> And the children of Israel journeyed from Rameses, and encamped in Succoth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And they journeyed from Succoth, and encamped in Etham, which is in the edge of the wilderness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And they journeyed from Etham, and turned back unto Pi-hahiroth, which is before Baal-zephon: and they encamped before Migdol.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And they journeyed from before Hahiroth, and passed through the midst of the sea into the wilderness: and they went three days’ journey in the wilderness of Etham, and encamped in Marah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> And they journeyed from Marah, and came unto Elim: and in Elim were twelve springs of water, and threescore and ten palm-trees; and they encamped there.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> And they journeyed from Elim, and encamped by the Red Sea.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> And they journeyed from the Red Sea, and encamped in the wilderness of Sin.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> And they journeyed from the wilderness of Sin, and encamped in Dophkah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> And they journeyed from Dophkah, and encamped in Alush.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> And they journeyed from Alush, and encamped in Rephidim, where was no water for the people to drink.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> And they journeyed from Rephidim, and encamped in the wilderness of Sinai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> And they journeyed from the wilderness of Sinai, and encamped in Kibroth-hattaavah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> And they journeyed from Kibroth-hattaavah, and encamped in Hazeroth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> And they journeyed from Hazeroth, and encamped in Rithmah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> And they journeyed from Rithmah, and encamped in Rimmon-perez.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> And they journeyed from Rimmon-perez, and encamped in Libnah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> And they journeyed from Libnah, and encamped in Rissah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> And they journeyed from Rissah, and encamped in Kehelathah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> And they journeyed from Kehelathah, and encamped in mount Shepher.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> And they journeyed from mount Shepher, and encamped in Haradah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> And they journeyed from Haradah, and encamped in Makheloth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> And they journeyed from Makheloth, and encamped in Tahath.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> And they journeyed from Tahath, and encamped in Terah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> And they journeyed from Terah, and encamped in Mithkah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> And they journeyed from Mithkah, and encamped in Hashmonah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> And they journeyed from Hashmonah, and encamped in Moseroth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> And they journeyed from Moseroth, and encamped in Bene-jaakan.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> And they journeyed from Bene-jaakan, and encamped in Hor-haggidgad.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">33</sup> And they journeyed from Hor-haggidgad, and encamped in Jotbathah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> And they journeyed from Jotbathah, and encamped in Abronah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">35</sup> And they journeyed from Abronah, and encamped in Ezion-geber.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">36</sup> And they journeyed from Ezion-geber, and encamped in the wilderness of Zin (the same is Kadesh).
<br>
<sup class="versenum">37</sup> And they journeyed from Kadesh, and encamped in mount Hor, in the edge of the land of Edom.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">38</sup> And Haron (Aaron) the priest went up into mount Hor at the commandment of تتراجراماتون, and died there, in the fortieth year after the children of Israel were come out of the land of Egypt, in the fifth month, on the first day of the month.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">39</sup> And Haron (Aaron) was a hundred and twenty and three years old when he died in mount Hor.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">40</sup> And the Canaanite, the king of Arad, who dwelt in the South in the land of Canaan, heard of the coming of the children of Israel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">41</sup> And they journeyed from mount Hor, and encamped in Zalmonah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">42</sup> And they journeyed from Zalmonah, and encamped in Punon.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">43</sup> And they journeyed from Punon, and encamped in Oboth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">44</sup> And they journeyed from Oboth, and encamped in Iye-abarim, in the border of Moab.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">45</sup> And they journeyed from Iyim, and encamped in Dibon-gad.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">46</sup> And they journeyed from Dibon-gad, and encamped in Almon-diblathaim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">47</sup> And they journeyed from Almon-diblathaim, and encamped in the mountains of Abarim, before Nebo.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">48</sup> And they journeyed from the mountains of Abarim, and encamped in the plains of Moab by the Jordan at Jericho.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">49</sup> And they encamped by the Jordan, from Beth-jeshimoth even unto Abel-shittim in the plains of Moab.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">50</sup> And تتراجراماتون spoke unto Musa in the plains of Moab by the Jordan at Jericho, saying, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">51</sup> Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye pass over the Jordan into the land of Canaan, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">52</sup> then ye shall drive out all the inhabitants of the land from before you, and destroy all their figured stones, and destroy all their molten images, and demolish all their high places: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">53</sup> and ye shall take possession of the land, and dwell therein; for unto you have I given the land to possess it.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">54</sup> And ye shall inherit the land by lot according to your families; to the more ye shall give the more inheritance, and to the fewer thou shalt give the less inheritance: wheresoever the lot falleth to any man, that shall be his; according to the tribes of your fathers shall ye inherit.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">55</sup> But if ye will not drive out the inhabitants of the land from before you, then shall those that ye let remain of them be as pricks in your eyes, and as thorns in your sides, and they shall vex you in the land wherein ye dwell.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">56</sup> And it shall come to pass, that, as I thought to do unto them, so will I do unto you.
<br>
<br>
</div>
