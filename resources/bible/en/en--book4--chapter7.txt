<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And it came to pass on the day that Musa had made an end of setting up the tabernacle, and had anointed it and sanctified it, and all the furniture thereof, and the altar and all the vessels thereof, and had anointed them and sanctified them;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> that the princes of Israel, the heads of their fathers’ houses, offered.
<br>
 These were the princes of the tribes, these are they that were over them that were numbered: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> and they brought their oblation before تتراجراماتون, six covered wagons, and twelve oxen; a wagon for every two of the princes, and for each one an ox: and they presented them before the tabernacle.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And تتراجراماتون spoke unto Musa, saying, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Take it of them, that they may be used in doing the service of the tent of meeting; and thou shalt give them unto the Levites, to every man according to his service.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And Musa took the wagons and the oxen, and gave them unto the Levites.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Two wagons and four oxen he gave unto the sons of Gershon, according to their service: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> and four wagons and eight oxen he gave unto the sons of Merari, according unto their service, under the hand of Ithamar the son of Haron (Aaron) the priest.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> But unto the sons of Kohath he gave none, because the service of the sanctuary belonged unto them; they bare it upon their shoulders.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> And the princes offered for the dedication of the altar in the day that it was anointed, even the princes offered their oblation before the altar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> And تتراجراماتون said unto Musa, They shall offer their oblation, each prince on his day, for the dedication of the altar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> And he that offered his oblation the first day was Nahshon the son of Amminadab, of the tribe of Judah: 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> and his oblation was one silver platter, the weight whereof was a hundred and thirty shekels, one silver bowl of seventy shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary; both of them full of fine flour mingled with oil for a meal-offering; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> one golden spoon of ten shekels, full of incense; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> one young bullock, one ram, one he-lamb a year old, for a burnt-offering; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> one male of the goats for a sin-offering; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> and for the sacrifice of peace-offerings, two oxen, five rams, five he-goats, five he-lambs a year old: this was the oblation of Nahshon the son of Amminadab.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> On the second day Nethanel the son of Zuar, prince of Issachar, did offer:

<sup class="versenum">84</sup> This was the dedication of the altar, in the day when it was anointed, by the princes of Israel: twelve silver platters, twelve silver bowls, twelve golden spoons; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">85</sup> each silver platter weighing a hundred and thirty shekels, and each bowl seventy; all the silver of the vessels two thousand and four hundred shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">86</sup> the twelve golden spoons, full of incense, weighing ten shekels apiece, after the shekel of the sanctuary; all the gold of the spoons a hundred and twenty shekels; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">87</sup> all the oxen for the burnt-offering twelve bullocks, the rams twelve, the he-lambs a year old twelve, and their meal-offering; and the males of the goats for a sin-offering twelve; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">88</sup> and all the oxen for the sacrifice of peace-offerings twenty and four bullocks, the rams sixty, the he-goats sixty, the he-lambs a year old sixty.
<br>
 This was the dedication of the altar, after that it was anointed.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">89</sup> And when Musa went into the tent of meeting to speak with him, then he heard the Voice speaking unto him from above the mercy-seat that was upon the ark of the testimony, from between the two cherubim: and he spoke unto him.
