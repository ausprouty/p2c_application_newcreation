<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Song of Ascents) In my distress I cried unto تتراجراماتون,
<br>
And he answered me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Deliver my soul, O تتراجراماتون, from lying lips,
<br>
And from a deceitful tongue.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> What shall be given unto thee, and what shall be done more unto thee,
<br>
Thou deceitful tongue?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Sharp arrows of the mighty,
<br>
With coals of juniper.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Woe is me, that I sojourn in Meshech,
<br>
That I dwell among the tents of Kedar!
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> My soul hath long had her dwelling
<br>
With him that hateth peace.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> I am for peace:
<br>
But when I speak, they are for war.
<br>
<br>
</div>
