<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> The sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> And the sons of Kohath: ‘Imran (Amram), Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> And the children of ‘Imran (Amram): Haron (Aaron), and Musa, and Miriam.
<br>
 And the sons of Haron (Aaron): Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Eleazar begat Phinehas, Phinehas begat Abishua, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> and Abishua begat Bukki, and Bukki begat Uzzi, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> and Uzzi begat Zerahiah, and Zerahiah begat Meraioth, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Meraioth begat Amariah, and Amariah begat Ahitub, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> and Ahitub begat Zadok, and Zadok begat Ahimaaz, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> and Ahimaaz begat Azariah, and Azariah begat Johanan, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> and Johanan begat Azariah (he it is that executed the priest’s office in the house that Solomon built in Jerusalem), 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> and Azariah begat Amariah, and Amariah begat Ahitub, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> and Ahitub begat Zadok, and Zadok begat Shallum, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> and Shallum begat Hilkiah, and Hilkiah begat Azariah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> and Azariah begat Seraiah, and Seraiah begat Jehozadak; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> And Jehozadak went into captivity, when تتراجراماتون carried away Judah and Jerusalem by the hand of Nebuchadnezzar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> The sons of Levi: Gershom, Kohath, and Merari.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> And these are the names of the sons of Gershom: Libni and Shimei.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> And the sons of Kohath were ‘Imran (Amram), and Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> The sons of Merari: Mahli and Mushi.
<br>
 And these are the families of the Levites according to their fathers’ houses.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> Of Gershom: Libni his son, Jahath his son, Zimmah his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> Joah his son, Iddo his son, Zerah his son, Jeatherai his son.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> The sons of Kohath: Amminadab his son, Korah his son, Assir his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> Elkanah his son, and Ebiasaph his son, and Assir his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> Tahath his son, Uriel his son, Uzziah his son, and Shaul his son.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> And the sons of Elkanah: Amasai, and Ahimoth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> As for Elkanah, the sons of Elkanah: Zophai his son, and Nahath his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> Eliab his son, Jeroham his son, Elkanah his son.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> And the sons of Samuel: the first-born, Joel, and the second Abijah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> The sons of Merari: Mahli, Libni his son, Shimei his son, Uzzah his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> Shimea his son, Haggiah his son, Asaiah his son.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> And these are they whom David (Dawad) set over the service of song in the house of تتراجراماتون, after that the ark had rest.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> And they ministered with song before the tabernacle of the tent of meeting, until Solomon had built the house of تتراجراماتون in Jerusalem: and they waited on their office according to their order.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">33</sup> And these are they that waited, and their sons.
<br>
 Of the sons of the Kohathites: Heman the singer, the son of Joel, the son of Samuel, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> the son of Elkanah, the son of Jeroham, the son of Eliel, the son of Toah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">35</sup> the son of Zuph, the son of Elkanah, the son of Mahath, the son of Amasai, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">36</sup> the son of Elkanah, the son of Joel, the son of Azariah, the son of Zephaniah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">37</sup> the son of Tahath, the son of Assir, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">38</sup> the son of Izhar, the son of Kohath, the son of Levi, the son of Israel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">39</sup> And his brother Asaph, who stood on his right hand, even Asaph the son of Berechiah, the son of Shimea, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">40</sup> the son of Michael, the son of Baaseiah, the son of Malchijah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">41</sup> the son of Ethni, the son of Zerah, the son of Adaiah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">42</sup> the son of Ethan, the son of Zimmah, the son of Shimei, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">43</sup> the son of Jahath, the son of Gershom, the son of Levi.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">44</sup> And on the left hand their brethren the sons of Merari: Ethan the son of Kishi, the son of Abdi, the son of Malluch, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">45</sup> the son of Hashabiah, the son of Amaziah, the son of Hilkiah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">46</sup> the son of Amzi, the son of Bani, the son of Shemer, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">47</sup> the son of Mahli, the son of Mushi, the son of Merari, the son of Levi.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">48</sup> And their brethren the Levites were appointed for all the service of the tabernacle of the house of Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">49</sup> But Haron (Aaron) and his sons offered upon the altar of burnt-offering, and upon the altar of incense, for all the work of the most holy place, and to make atonement for Israel, according to all that Musa the servant of Elokim had commanded.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">50</sup> And these are the sons of Haron (Aaron): Eleazar his son, Phinehas his son, Abishua his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">51</sup> Bukki his son, Uzzi his son, Zerahiah his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">52</sup> Meraioth his son, Amariah his son, Ahitub his son, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">53</sup> Zadok his son, Ahimaaz his son.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">54</sup> Now these are their dwelling-places according to their encampments in their borders: to the sons of Haron (Aaron), of the families of the Kohathites (for theirs was the first lot), 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">55</sup> to them they gave Hebron in the land of Judah, and the suburbs thereof round about it; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">56</sup> but the fields of the city, and the villages thereof, they gave to Caleb the son of Jephunneh.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">57</sup> And to the sons of Haron (Aaron) they gave the cities of refuge, Hebron; Libnah also with its suburbs, and Jattir, and Eshtemoa with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">58</sup> and Hilen with its suburbs, Debir with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">59</sup> and Ashan with its suburbs, and Beth-shemesh with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">60</sup> and out of the tribe of Benjamin, Geba with its suburbs, and Allemeth with its suburbs, and Anathoth with its suburbs.
<br>
 All their cities throughout their families were thirteen cities.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">61</sup> And unto the rest of the sons of Kohath were given by lot, out of the family of the tribe, out of the half-tribe, the half of Manasseh, ten cities.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">62</sup> And to the sons of Gershom, according to their families, out of the tribe of Issachar, and out of the tribe of Asher, and out of the tribe of Naphtali, and out of the tribe of Manasseh in Bashan, thirteen cities.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">63</sup> Unto the sons of Merari were given by lot, according to their families, out of the tribe of Reuben, and out of the tribe of Gad, and out of the tribe of Zebulun, twelve cities.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">64</sup> And the children of Israel gave to the Levites the cities with their suburbs.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">65</sup> And they gave by lot out of the tribe of the children of Judah, and out of the tribe of the children of Simeon, and out of the tribe of the children of Benjamin, these cities which are mentioned by name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">66</sup> And some of the families of the sons of Kohath had cities of their borders out of the tribe of Ephraim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">67</sup> And they gave unto them the cities of refuge, Shechem in the hill-country of Ephraim with its suburbs; Gezer also with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">68</sup> and Jokmeam with its suburbs, and Beth-horon with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">69</sup> and Aijalon with its suburbs, and Gath-rimmon with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">70</sup> and out of the half-tribe of Manasseh, Aner with its suburbs, and Bileam with its suburbs, for the rest of the family of the sons of Kohath.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">71</sup> Unto the sons of Gershom were given, out of the family of the half-tribe of Manasseh, Golan in Bashan with its suburbs, and Ashtaroth with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">72</sup> and out of the tribe of Issachar, Kedesh with its suburbs, Daberath with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">73</sup> and Ramoth with its suburbs, and Anem with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">74</sup> and out of the tribe of Asher, Mashal with its suburbs, and Abdon with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">75</sup> and Hukok with its suburbs, and Rehob with its suburbs;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">76</sup> and out of the tribe of Naphtali, Kedesh in Galilee with its suburbs, and Hammon with its suburbs, and Kiriathaim with its suburbs.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">77</sup></sup> Unto the rest of the Levites, the sons of Merari, were given, out of the tribe of Zebulun, Rimmono with its suburbs, Tabor with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">78</sup> and beyond the Jordan at Jericho, on the east side of the Jordan, were given them, out of the tribe of Reuben, Bezer in the wilderness with its suburbs, and Jahzah with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">79</sup> and Kedemoth with its suburbs, and Mephaath with its suburbs; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">80</sup> and out of the tribe of Gad, Ramoth in Gilead with its suburbs, and Mahanaim with its suburbs, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">81</sup> and Heshbon with its suburbs, and Jazer with its suburbs.
