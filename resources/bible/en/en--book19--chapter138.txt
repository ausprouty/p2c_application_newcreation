<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Psalm of Dawad) I will give thee thanks with my whole heart:
<br>
Before the Elokim will I sing praises unto thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> I will worship toward thy holy temple,
<br>
And give thanks unto thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth:
<br>
For thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> In the day that I called thou answeredst me,
<br>
Thou didst encourage me with strength in my soul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> All the kings of the earth shall give thee thanks, O تتراجراماتون,
<br>
For they have heard the words of thy mouth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Yea, they shall sing of the ways of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
For great is the glory of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> For though تتراجراماتون is high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly;
<br>
But the haughty he knoweth from afar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me;
<br>
Thou wilt stretch forth thy hand against the wrath of mine enemies,
<br>
And thy right hand will save me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> تتراجراماتون will perfect that which concerneth me:
<br>
Thy lovingkindness, O تتراجراماتون, endureth for ever;
<br>
Forsake not the works of thine own hands.
<br>
<br>
</div>
