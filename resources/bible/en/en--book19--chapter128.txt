<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Song of Ascents) Blessed is every one that feareth تتراجراماتون,
<br>
That walketh in his ways.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> For thou shalt eat the labor of thy hands:
<br>
Happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine,
<br>
In the innermost parts of thy house;
<br>
Thy children like olive plants,
<br>
Round about thy table.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Behold, thus shall the man be blessed
<br>
That feareth تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> تتراجراماتون bless thee out of Zion:
<br>
And see thou the good of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Yea, see thou thy children’s children.
<br>
Peace be upon Israel.
<br>
<br>
</div>
