<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter31.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(A Psalm; a Song at the Dedication of the House. A Psalm of Dawad) I will extol thee, O تتراجراماتون; for thou hast raised me up,
<br>
And hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> O تتراجراماتون my Elokim,
<br>
I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> O تتراجراماتون, thou hast brought up my soul from Sheol;
<br>
Thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Sing praise unto تتراجراماتون, O ye saints of his,
<br>
And give thanks to his holy memorial name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> For his anger is but for a moment;
<br>
His favor is for a life-time:
<br>
Weeping may tarry for the night,
<br>
But joy cometh in the morning.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> As for me, I said in my prosperity,
<br>
I shall never be moved.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Thou, تتراجراماتون, of thy favor hadst made my mountain to stand strong:
<br>
Thou didst hide thy face; I was troubled.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> I cried to thee, O تتراجراماتون;
<br>
And unto تتراجراماتون I made supplication:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> What profit is there in my blood, when I go down to the pit?
<br>
Shall the dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> Hear, O تتراجراماتون, and have mercy upon me:
<br>
تتراجراماتون, be thou my helper.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing;
<br>
Thou hast loosed my sackcloth, and girded me with gladness;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> To the end that my glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent.
<br>
O تتراجراماتون my Elokim, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.
<br>
<br>
</div>
