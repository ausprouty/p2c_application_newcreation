<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter85.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 86</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter87.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(A Prayer of Dawad) Bow down thine ear, O تتراجراماتون, and answer me;
<br>
For I am poor and needy.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Preserve my soul; for I am godly:
<br>
O thou my Elokim, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Be merciful unto me, O Lord;
<br>
For unto thee do I cry all the day long.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Rejoice the soul of thy servant;
<br>
For unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive,
<br>
And abundant in lovingkindness unto all them that call upon thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Give ear, O تتراجراماتون, unto my prayer;
<br>
And hearken unto the voice of my supplications.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee;
<br>
For thou wilt answer me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> There is none like unto thee among the Elokim, O Lord;
<br>
Neither are there any works like unto thy works.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord;
<br>
And they shall glorify thy name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> For thou art great, and doest wondrous things:
<br>
Thou art Elokim alone.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Teach me thy way, O تتراجراماتون; I will walk in thy truth:
<br>
Unite my heart to fear thy name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> I will praise thee, O Lord my Elokim, with my whole heart;
<br>
And I will glorify thy name for evermore.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> For great is thy lovingkindness toward me;
<br>
And thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> O Elokim, the proud are risen up against me,
<br>
And a company of violent men have sought after my soul,
<br>
And have not set thee before them.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> But thou, O Lord, art a Elokim merciful and gracious,
<br>
Slow to anger, and abundant in lovingkindness and truth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> Oh turn unto me, and have mercy upon me;
<br>
Give thy strength unto thy servant,
<br>
And save the son of thy handmaid.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> Show me a token for good,
<br>
That they who hate me may see it, and be put to shame,
<br>
Because thou, تتراجراماتون, hast helped me, and comforted me.
<br>
<br>
</div>
