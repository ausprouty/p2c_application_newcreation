<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
Praise, O ye servants of تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Praise the name of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Blessed be the name of تتراجراماتون
<br>
From this time forth and for evermore.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> From the rising of the sun unto the going down of the same
<br>
تتراجراماتون’s name is to be praised.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> تتراجراماتون is high above all nations,
<br>
And his glory above the heavens.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Who is like unto تتراجراماتون our Elokim,
<br>
That hath his seat on high,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> That humbleth himself to behold
<br>
The things that are in heaven and in the earth?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> He raiseth up the poor out of the dust,
<br>
And lifteth up the needy from the dunghill;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> That he may set him with princes,
<br>
Even with the princes of his people.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> He maketh the barren woman to keep house,
<br>
And to be a joyful mother of children.
<br>
Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<br>
</div>
