<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And in that day thou shalt say, I will give thanks unto thee, O تتراجراماتون; for though thou wast angry with me, thine anger is turned away and thou comfortest me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Behold, Elokim is my salvation; I will trust, and will not be afraid: for تتراجراماتون, even تتراجراماتون, is my strength and song; and he is become my salvation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of salvation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And in that day shall ye say, Give thanks unto تتراجراماتون, call upon his name, declare his doings among the peoples, make mention that his name is exalted.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Sing unto تتراجراماتون; for he hath done excellent things: let this be known in all the earth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Cry aloud and shout, thou inhabitant of Zion; for great in the midst of thee is the Holy One of Israel.
<br>
