<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter144.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 145</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter146.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Psalm of praise; of Dawad) I will extol thee, my Elokim, O King; And I will bless thy name for ever and ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Every day will I bless thee;
<br>
And I will praise thy name for ever and ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Great is تتراجراماتون, and greatly to be praised;
<br>
And his greatness is unsearchable.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> One generation shall laud thy works to another,
<br>
And shall declare thy mighty acts.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Of the glorious majesty of thine honor,
<br>
And of thy wondrous works, will I meditate.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts;
<br>
And I will declare thy greatness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> They shall utter the memory of thy great goodness,
<br>
And shall sing of thy righteousness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> تتراجراماتون is gracious, and merciful;
<br>
Slow to anger, and of great lovingkindness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> تتراجراماتون is good to all;
<br>
And his tender mercies are over all his works.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> All thy works shall give thanks unto thee, O تتراجراماتون;
<br>
And thy saints shall bless thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom,
<br>
And talk of thy power;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts,
<br>
And the glory of the majesty of his kingdom.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom,
<br>
And thy dominion endureth throughout all generations.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> تتراجراماتون upholdeth all that fall,
<br>
And raiseth up all those that are bowed down.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> The eyes of all wait for thee;
<br>
And thou givest them their food in due season.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> Thou openest thy hand,
<br>
And satisfiest the desire of every living thing.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> تتراجراماتون is righteous in all his ways,
<br>
And gracious in all his works.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> تتراجراماتون is nigh unto all them that call upon him,
<br>
To all that call upon him in truth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him;
<br>
He also will hear their cry and will save them.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> تتراجراماتون preserveth all them that love him;
<br>
But all the wicked will he destroy.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> My mouth shall speak the praise of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
And let all flesh bless his holy name for ever and ever.[T.N. See Messianic Psalms which include Ps 2, 22, 45, 72,110, 47, 93, 96-99, 89 and which indicate his righteous character, experience, ideals, call and reign.]
<br>
<br>
</div>
