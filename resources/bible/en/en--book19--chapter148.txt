<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter147.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 148</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter149.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
Praise ye تتراجراماتون from the heavens:
<br>
Praise him in the heights.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Praise ye him, all his angels:
<br>
Praise ye him, all his host.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Praise ye him, sun and moon:
<br>
Praise him, all ye stars of light.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Praise him, ye heavens of heavens,
<br>
And ye waters that are above the heavens.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Let them praise the name of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
For he commanded, and they were created.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> He hath also established them for ever and ever:
<br>
He hath made a decree which shall not pass away.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Praise تتراجراماتون from the earth,
<br>
Ye sea-monsters, and all deeps;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> Fire and hail, snow and vapor;
<br>
Stormy wind, fulfilling his word;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> Mountains and all hills;
<br>
Fruitful trees and all cedars;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> Beasts and all cattle;
<br>
Creeping things and flying birds;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Kings of the earth and all peoples;
<br>
Princes and all judges of the earth;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> Both young men and virgins;
<br>
Old men and children:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> Let them praise the name of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
For his name alone is exalted;
<br>
His glory is above the earth and the heavens.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> And he hath lifted up the horn of his people,
<br>
The praise of all his saints;
<br>
Even of the children of Israel, a people near unto him.
<br>
Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<br>
</div>
