<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(For the Chief Musician. A Psalm of Dawad) تتراجراماتون answer thee in the day of trouble;
<br>
The name of the Elokim of Ya’qub (Jacob) set thee up on high;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Send thee help from the sanctuary,
<br>
And strengthen thee out of Zion;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Remember all thy offerings,
<br>
And accept thy burnt-sacrifice;
<br>
Selah
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Grant thee thy heart’s desire,
<br>
And fulfil all thy counsel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> We will triumph in thy salvation,
<br>
And in the name of our Elokim we will set up our banners:
<br>
تتراجراماتون fulfil all thy petitions.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Now know I that تتراجراماتون saveth his anointed;
<br>
He will answer him from his holy heaven
<br>
With the saving strength of his right hand.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Some trust in chariots, and some in horses;
<br>
But we will make mention of the name of تتراجراماتون our Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> They are bowed down and fallen;
<br>
But we are risen, and stand upright.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> Save, تتراجراماتون:
<br>
Let the King answer us when we call.
<br>
<br>
</div>
