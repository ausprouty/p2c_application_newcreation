<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> These are the sons of Israel: Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Dan, Yusuf (Joseph), and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> The sons of Judah: Er, and Onan, and Shelah; which three were born unto him of Shua’s daughter the Canaanitess.
<br>
 And Er, Judah’s first-born, was wicked in the sight of تتراجراماتون; and he slew him.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And Tamar his daughter-in-law bare him Perez and Zerah.
<br>
 All the sons of Judah were five.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> The sons of Perez: Hezron, and Hamul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> And the sons of Zerah: Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara; five of them in all.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And the sons of Carmi: Achar, the troubler of Israel, who committed a trespass in the devoted thing.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And the sons of Ethan: Azariah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> The sons also of Hezron, that were born unto him: Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> And Ram begat Amminadab, and Amminadab begat Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> and Nahshon begat Salma, and Salma begat Boaz, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> and Boaz begat Obed, and Obed begat Jesse; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> and Jesse begat his first-born Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimea the third, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> Nethanel the fourth, Raddai the fifth, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> Ozem the sixth, David (Dawad) the seventh; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> and their sisters were Zeruiah and Abigail.
<br>
 And the sons of Zeruiah: Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.
<br>
 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> And Abigail bare Amasa; and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmaelite.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> And Caleb the son of Hezron begat children of Azubah his wife, and of Jerioth; and these were her sons: Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> And Azubah died, and Caleb took unto him Ephrath, who bare him Hur.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> And Hur begat Uri, and Uri begat Bezalel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> And afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he took to wife when he was threescore years old; and she bare him Segub.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> And Segub begat Jair, who had three and twenty cities in the land of Gilead.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> And Geshur and Aram took the towns of Jair from them, with Kenath, and the villages thereof, even threescore cities.
<br>
 All these were the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> And after that Hezron was dead in Caleb-ephrathah, then Abijah Hezron’s wife bare him Ashhur the father of Tekoa.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> And the sons of Jerahmeel the first-born of Hezron were Ram the first-born, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, Ahijah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> And Jerahmeel had another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> And the sons of Ram the first-born of Jerahmeel were Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> And the sons of Onam were Shammai, and Jada.
<br>
 And the sons of Shammai: Nadab, and Abishur.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> And the name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail; and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> And the sons of Nadab: Seled, and Appaim; but Seled died without children.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> And the sons of Appaim: Ishi.
<br>
 And the sons of Ishi: Sheshan.
<br>
 And the sons of Sheshan: Ahlai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai: Jether, and Jonathan; and Jether died without children.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">33</sup> And the sons of Jonathan: Peleth, and Zaza.
<br>
 These were the sons of Jerahmeel.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters.
<br>
 And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">35</sup> And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">36</sup> And Attai begat Nathan, and Nathan begat Zabad, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">37</sup> and Zabad begat Ephlal, and Ephlal begat Obed, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">38</sup> and Obed begat Jehu, and Jehu begat Azariah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">39</sup> and Azariah begat Helez, and Helez begat Eleasah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">40</sup> and Eleasah begat Sismai, and Sismai begat Shallum, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">41</sup> and Shallum begat Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat Elishama.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">42</sup> And the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were Mesha his first-born, who was the father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">43</sup> And the sons of Hebron: Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">44</sup> And Shema begat Raham, the father of Jorkeam; and Rekem begat Shammai.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">45</sup> And the son of Shammai was Maon; and Maon was the father of Beth-zur.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">46</sup> And Ephah, Caleb’s concubine, bare Haran, and Moza, and Gazez; and Haran begat Gazez.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">47</sup> And the sons of Jahdai: Regem, and Jothan, and Geshan, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">48</sup> Maacah, Caleb’s concubine, bare Sheber and Tirhanah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">49</sup> She bare also Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbena, and the father of Gibea; and the daughter of Caleb was Achsah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">50</sup> These were the sons of Caleb, the son of Hur, the first-born of Ephrathah: Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">51</sup> Salma the father of Beth-lehem, Hareph the father of Beth-gader.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">52</sup> And Shobal the father of Kiriath-jearim had sons: Haroeh, half of the Menuhoth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">53</sup> And the families of Kiriath-jearim: The Ithrites, and the Puthites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zorathites and the Eshtaolites.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">54</sup> The sons of Salma: Beth-lehem, and the Netophathites, Atroth-beth-joab, and half of the Manahathites, the Zorites.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">55</sup> And the families of scribes that dwelt at Jabez: the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, the Sucathites. These are the Kenites that came of Hammath, the father of the house of Rechab.
<br>
<br>
