<p><a href = "#chapter/book16--chapter10.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Nehemiah 11</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book16--chapter12.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> And the princes of the people dwelt in Jerusalem: the rest of the people also cast lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in Jerusalem the holy city, and nine parts in the other cities.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> And the people blessed all the men that willingly offered themselves to dwell in Jerusalem.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Now these are the chiefs of the province that dwelt in Jerusalem: but in the cities of Judah dwelt every one in his possession in their cities, to wit, Israel, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinim, and the children of Solomon’s servants.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> And in Jerusalem dwelt certain of the children of Judah, and of the children of Benjamin.
<br>
 Of the children of Judah: Athaiah the son of Uzziah, the son of Zechariah, the son of Amariah, the son of Shephatiah, the son of Mahalalel, of the children of Perez; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> and Maaseiah the son of Baruch, the son of Colhozeh, the son of Hazaiah, the son of Adaiah, the son of Joiarib, the son of Zechariah, the son of the Shilonite.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> All the sons of Perez that dwelt in Jerusalem were four hundred threescore and eight valiant men.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> And these are the sons of Benjamin: Sallu the son of Meshullam, the son of Joed, the son of Pedaiah, the son of Kolaiah, the son of Maaseiah, the son of Ithiel, the son of Jeshaiah.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And after him Gabbai, Sallai, nine hundred twenty and eight.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> And Joel the son of Zichri was their overseer; and Judah the son of Hassenuah was second over the city.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> Of the priests: Jedaiah the son of Joiarib, Jachin, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Seraiah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok, the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, the ruler of the house of Elokim, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> and their brethren that did the work of the house, eight hundred twenty and two; and Adaiah the son of Jeroham, the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi, the son of Zechariah, the son of Pashhur, the son of Malchijah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> and his brethren, chiefs of fathers’ houses, two hundred forty and two; and Amashsai the son of Azarel, the son of Ahzai, the son of Meshillemoth, the son of Immer, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> and their brethren, mighty men of valor, a hundred twenty and eight; and their overseer was Zabdiel, the son of Haggedolim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> And of the Levites: Shemaiah the son of Hasshub, the son of Azrikam, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Bunni; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> and Shabbethai and Jozabad, of the chiefs of the Levites, who had the oversight of the outward business of the house of Elokim; 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> and Mattaniah the son of Mica, the son of Zabdi, the son of Asaph, who was the chief to begin the thanksgiving in prayer, and Bakbukiah, the second among his brethren; and Abda the son of Shammua, the son of Galal, the son of Jeduthun.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> All the Levites in the holy city were two hundred fourscore and four.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> Moreover the porters, Akkub, Talmon, and their brethren, that kept watch at the gates, were a hundred seventy and two.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> And the residue of Israel, of the priests, the Levites, were in all the cities of Judah, every one in his inheritance.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> But the Nethinim dwelt in Ophel: and Ziha and Gishpa were over the Nethinim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> The overseer also of the Levites at Jerusalem was Uzzi the son of Bani, the son of Hashabiah, the son of Mattaniah, the son of Mica, of the sons of Asaph, the singers, over the business of the house of Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> For there was a commandment from the king concerning them, and a settled provision for the singers, as every day required.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> And Pethahiah the son of Meshezabel, of the children of Zerah the son of Judah, was at the king’s hand in all matters concerning the people.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> And as for the villages, with their fields, some of the children of Judah dwelt in Kiriath-arba and the towns thereof, and in Dibon and the towns thereof, and in Jekabzeel and the villages thereof, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> and in Jeshua, and in Moladah, and Beth-pelet, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> and in Hazar-shual, and in Beer-sheba and the towns thereof, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> and in Ziklag, and in Meconah and in the towns thereof, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> and in En-rimmon, and in Zorah, and in Jarmuth, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> Zanoah, Adullam, and their villages, Lachish and the fields thereof, Azekah and the towns thereof.
<br>
 So they encamped from Beer-sheba unto the valley of Hinnom.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> The children of Benjamin also dwelt from Geba onward, at Michmash and Aija, and at Beth-el and the towns thereof, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> at Anathoth, Nob, Ananiah, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">33</sup> Hazor, Ramah, Gittaim,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> Hadid, Zeboim, Neballat, 
<br>
<sup class="versenum">35</sup> Lod, and Ono, the valley of craftsmen.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">36</sup> And of the Levites, certain courses in Judah were joined to Benjamin.
<br>
<br>
</div>
