<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Psalm of thanksgiving) Make a joyful noise unto تتراجراماتون, all ye lands.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Serve تتراجراماتون with gladness:
<br>
Come before his presence with singing.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Know ye that تتراجراماتون, he is Elokim:
<br>
It is he that hath made us, and we are his;
<br>
We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Enter into his gates with thanksgiving,
<br>
And into his courts with praise:
<br>
Give thanks unto him, and bless his name.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> For تتراجراماتون is good; his lovingkindness endureth for ever,
<br>
And his faithfulness unto all generations.
<br>
<br>
</div>
