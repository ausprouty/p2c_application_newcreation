<sup class="versenum">1</sup> ALEF Blessed are they that are perfect in the way,
<br>
Who walk in the law of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Blessed are they that keep his testimonies,
<br>
That seek him with the whole heart.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Yea, they do no unrighteousness;
<br>
They walk in his ways.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Thou hast commanded us thy precepts,
<br>
That we should observe them diligently.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Oh that my ways were established
<br>
To observe thy statutes!
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Then shall I not be put to shame,
<br>
When I have respect unto all thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> I will give thanks unto thee with uprightness of heart,
<br>
When I learn thy righteous judgments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> I will observe thy statutes<sup class="versenum">
<br>
Oh forsake me not utterly.

<br>
By taking heed thereto according to thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> With my whole heart have I sought thee:
<br>
Oh let me not wander from thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> Thy word have I laid up in my heart,
<br>
That I might not sin against thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> Blessed art thou, O تتراجراماتون:
<br>
Teach me thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> With my lips have I declared
<br>
All the ordinances of thy mouth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> I have rejoiced in the way of thy testimonies,
<br>
As much as in all riches.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> I will meditate on thy precepts,
<br>
And have respect unto thy ways.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> I will delight myself in thy statutes:
<br>
I will not forget thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> GIMEL Deal bountifully with thy servant, that I may live;
<br>
So will I observe thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> Open thou mine eyes, that I may behold
<br>
Wondrous things out of thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> I am a sojourner in the earth:
<br>
Hide not thy commandments from me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> My soul breaketh for the longing
<br>
That it hath unto thine ordinances at all times.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> Thou hast rebuked the proud that are cursed,
<br>
That do wander from thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> Take away from me reproach and contempt;
<br>
For I have kept thy testimonies.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> Princes also sat and talked against me;
<br>
But thy servant did meditate on thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> Thy testimonies also are my delight
<br>
And my counsellors.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> DALET My soul cleaveth unto the dust:
<br>
Quicken thou me according to thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> I declared my ways, and thou answeredst me:
<br>
Teach me thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> Make me to understand the way of thy precepts:
<br>
So shall I meditate on thy wondrous works.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> My soul melteth for heaviness<sup class="versenum">
<br>
Strengthen thou me according unto thy word.

<sup class="versenum">29</sup> Remove from me the way of falsehood;
<br>
And grant me thy law graciously.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">30</sup> I have chosen the way of faithfulness:
<br>
Thine ordinances have I set before me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">31</sup> I cleave unto thy testimonies:
<br>
O تتراجراماتون, put me not to shame.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">32</sup> I will run the way of thy commandments,
<br>
When thou shalt enlarge my heart.

<sup class="versenum">33</sup>HEH Teach me, O تتراجراماتون, the way of thy statutes;
<br>
And I shall keep it unto the end.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">34</sup> Give me understanding, and I shall keep thy law;
<br>
Yea, I shall observe it with my whole heart.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">35</sup> Make me to go in the path of thy commandments;
<br>
For therein do I delight.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">36</sup> Incline my heart unto thy testimonies,
<br>
And not to covetousness.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">37</sup> Turn away mine eyes from beholding vanity,
<br>
And quicken me in thy ways.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">38</sup> Confirm unto thy servant thy word,
<br>
Which is in order unto the fear of thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">39</sup> Turn away my reproach whereof I am afraid;
<br>
For thine ordinances are good.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">40</sup> Behold, I have longed after thy precepts:
<br>
Quicken me in thy righteousness.

<sup class="versenum">41</sup> VAV Let thy lovingkindnesses also come unto me, O تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Even thy salvation, according to thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">42</sup> So shall I have an answer for him that reproacheth me;
<br>
For I trust in thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">43</sup> And take not the word of truth utterly out of my mouth;
<br>
For I have hoped in thine ordinances.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">44</sup> So shall I observe thy law continually
<br>
For ever and ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">45</sup> And I shall walk at liberty;
<br>
For I have sought thy precepts.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">46</sup> I will also speak of thy testimonies before kings,
<br>
And shall not be put to shame.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">47</sup> And I will delight myself in thy commandments,
<br>
Which I have loved.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">48</sup> I will lift up my hands also unto thy commandments, which I have loved;
<br>
And I will meditate on thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">49</sup>ZAYIN Remember the word unto thy servant,
<br>
Because thou hast made me to hope.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">50</sup> This is my comfort in my affliction;
<br>
For thy word hath quickened me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">51</sup> The proud have had me greatly in derision:
<br>
Yet have I not swerved from thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">52</sup> I have remembered thine ordinances of old, O تتراجراماتون,
<br>
And have comforted myself.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">53</sup> Hot indignation hath taken hold upon me,
<br>
Because of the wicked that forsake thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">54</sup> Thy statutes have been my songs
<br>
In the house of my pilgrimage.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">55</sup> I have remembered thy name, O تتراجراماتون, in the night,
<br>
And have observed thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">56</sup> This I have had,
<br>
Because I have kept thy precepts.
<br>

<br>
I have said that I would observe thy words.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">58</sup> I entreated thy favor with my whole heart:
<br>
Be merciful unto me according to thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">59</sup> I thought on my ways,
<br>
And turned my feet unto thy testimonies.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">60</sup> I made haste, and delayed not,
<br>
To observe thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">61</sup> The cords of the wicked have wrapped me round;
<br>
But I have not forgotten thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">62</sup> At midnight I will rise to give thanks unto thee
<br>
Because of thy righteous ordinances.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">63</sup> I am a companion of all them that fear thee,
<br>
And of them that observe thy precepts.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">64</sup> The earth, O تتراجراماتون, is full of thy lovingkindness:
<br>
Teach me thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">65</sup> TET Thou hast dealt well with thy servant,
<br>
O تتراجراماتون, according unto thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">66</sup> Teach me good judgment and knowledge;
<br>
For I have believed in thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">67</sup> Before I was afflicted I went astray;
<br>
But now I observe thy word.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">68</sup> Thou art good, and doest good;
<br>
Teach me thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">69</sup> The proud have forged a lie against me:
<br>
With my whole heart will I keep thy precepts.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">70</sup> Their heart is as fat as grease;
<br>
But I delight in thy law.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">71</sup> It is good for me that I have been afflicted;
<br>
That I may learn thy statutes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">72</sup> The law of thy mouth is better unto me
<br>
Than thousands of gold and silver.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">73</sup> YOD Thy hands have made me and fashioned me:
<br>
Give me understanding, that I may learn thy commandments.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">74</sup> They that fear thee shall see me and be glad,
<br>
Because I have hoped in thy word.

<sup class="versenum">77</sup></sup> Let thy tender mercies come unto me, that I may live;

For thy law is my delight.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">78</sup> Let the proud be put to shame;

<br>

<sup class="versenum">80</sup> Let my heart be perfect in thy statutes,
