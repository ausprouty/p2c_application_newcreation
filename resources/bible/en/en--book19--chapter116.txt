<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter115.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 116</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter117.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> I love تتراجراماتون, because he heareth
<br>
My voice and my supplications.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Because he hath inclined his ear unto me,
<br>
Therefore will I call upon him as long as I live.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> The cords of death compassed me,
<br>
And the pains of Sheol gat hold upon me:
<br>
I found trouble and sorrow.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Then called I upon the name of تتراجراماتون:
<br>
O تتراجراماتون, I beseech thee, deliver my soul.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Gracious is تتراجراماتون, and righteous;
<br>
Yea, our Elokim is merciful.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> تتراجراماتون preserveth the simple:
<br>
I was brought low, and he saved me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Return unto thy rest, O my soul;
<br>
For تتراجراماتون hath dealt bountifully with thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> For thou hast delivered my soul from death,
<br>
Mine eyes from tears,
<br>
And my feet from falling.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> I will walk before تتراجراماتون
<br>
In the land of the living.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> I believe, for I will speak:
<br>
I was greatly afflicted:
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> I said in my haste,
<br>
All men are liars.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> What shall I render unto تتراجراماتون
<br>
For all his benefits toward me?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> I will take the cup of salvation,
<br>
And call upon the name of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> I will pay my vows unto تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Yea, in the presence of all his people.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> Precious in the sight of تتراجراماتون
<br>
Is the death of his saints.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> O تتراجراماتون, truly I am thy servant:
<br>
I am thy servant, the son of thy handmaid;
<br>
Thou hast loosed my bonds.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> I will offer to thee the sacrifice of thanksgiving,
<br>
And will call upon the name of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> I will pay my vows unto تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Yea, in the presence of all his people,
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> In the courts of تتراجراماتون’s house,
<br>
In the midst of thee, O Jerusalem.
<br>
Praise ye تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<br>
</div>
