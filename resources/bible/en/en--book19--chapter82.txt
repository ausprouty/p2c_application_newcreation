<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(A Psalm of Asaph) Elokim standeth in the congregation of Elokim;
<br>
He judgeth among the Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> How long will ye judge unjustly,
<br>
And respect the persons of the wicked?
<br>
Selah
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Judge the poor and fatherless:
<br>
Do justice to the afflicted and destitute.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Rescue the poor and needy:
<br>
Deliver them out of the hand of the wicked.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> They know not, neither do they understand;
<br>
They walk to and fro in darkness:
<br>
All the foundations of the earth are shaken.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> I said, Ye are Elokim,
<br>
And all of you sons of the Most High.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> Nevertheless ye shall die like men,
<br>
And fall like one of the princes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> Arise, O Elokim, judge the earth;
<br>
For thou shalt inherit all the nations.
<br>
<br>
</div>
