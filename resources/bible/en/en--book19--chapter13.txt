<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> (For the Chief Musician. A Psalm of Dawad). How long, O تتراجراماتون? wilt thou forget me for ever?
<br>
How long wilt thou hide thy face from me?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> How long shall I take counsel in my soul,
<br>
Having sorrow in my heart all the day?
<br>
How long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Consider and answer me, O تتراجراماتون my Elokim:
<br>
Lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him;
<br>
Lest mine adversaries rejoice when I am moved.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> But I have trusted in thy lovingkindness;
<br>
My heart shall rejoice in thy salvation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> I will sing unto تتراجراماتون,
<br>
Because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
<br>
<br>
</div>
