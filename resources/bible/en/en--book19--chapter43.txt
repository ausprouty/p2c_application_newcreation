<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter44.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Judge me, O Elokim, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation:
<br>
Oh deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> For thou art the Elokim of my strength; why hast thou cast me off?
<br>
Why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Oh send out thy light and thy truth; let them lead me:
<br>
Let them bring me unto thy holy hill,
<br>
And to thy tabernacles.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Then will I go unto the altar of Elokim,
<br>
Unto Elokim my exceeding joy;
<br>
And upon the harp will I praise thee, O Elokim, my Elokim.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Why art thou cast down, O my soul?
<br>
And why art thou disquieted within me?
<br>
Hope thou in Elokim; for I shall yet praise him,
<br>
Who is the help of my countenance, and my Elokim.
<br>
<br>
</div>
