<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter131.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup></sup>(A Song of Ascents) Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Lord, hear my voice:
<br>
Let thine ears be attentive
<br>
To the voice of my supplications.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> If thou, تتراجراماتون, shouldest mark iniquities,
<br>
O Lord, who could stand?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> But there is forgiveness with thee,
<br>
That thou mayest be feared.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> I wait for تتراجراماتون, my soul doth wait,
<br>
And in his word do I hope.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> My soul waiteth for HaAdon
<br>
More than watchmen wait for the morning;
<br>
Yea, more than watchmen for the morning.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> O Israel, hope in تتراجراماتون;
<br>
For with تتراجراماتون there is lovingkindness,
<br>
And with him is plenteous redemption.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> And he will redeem Israel
<br>
From all his iniquities.
<br>
<br>
</div>
