<p><a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter117.txt"><span class="icon icon-back"></span></a><span class="bible_book">Psalm 118</span>
<a href = "#chapter/book19--chapter119.txt"><span class="icon icon-forward"></span></a></p>
<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup> Oh give thanks unto تتراجراماتون; for he is good;
<br>
For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> Let Israel now say,
<br>
That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> Let the house of Haron (Aaron) now say,
<br>
That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> Let them now that fear تتراجراماتون say,
<br>
That his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Out of my distress I called upon تتراجراماتون:
<br>
تتراجراماتون answered me and set me in a large place.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> تتراجراماتون is on my side; I will not fear:
<br>
What can man do unto me?
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> تتراجراماتون is on my side among them that help me:
<br>
Therefore shall I see my desire upon them that hate me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> It is better to take refuge in تتراجراماتون
<br>
Than to put confidence in man.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> It is better to take refuge in تتراجراماتون
<br>
Than to put confidence in princes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">10</sup> All nations compassed me about:
<br>
In the name of تتراجراماتون I will cut them off.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">11</sup> They compassed me about; yea, they compassed me about:
<br>
In the name of تتراجراماتون I will cut them off.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">12</sup> They compassed me about like bees;
<br>
They are quenched as the fire of thorns:
<br>
In the name of تتراجراماتون I will cut them off.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">13</sup> Thou didst thrust sore at me that I might fall;
<br>
But تتراجراماتون helped me.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">14</sup> تتراجراماتون is my strength and song;
<br>
And he is become my salvation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">15</sup> The voice of rejoicing and salvation is in the tents of the righteous:
<br>
The right hand of تتراجراماتون doeth valiantly.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">16</sup> The right hand of تتراجراماتون is exalted:
<br>
The right hand of تتراجراماتون doeth valiantly.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">17</sup> I shall not die, but live,
<br>
And declare the works of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">18</sup> تتراجراماتون hath chastened me sore;
<br>
But he hath not given me over unto death.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">19</sup> Open to me the gates of righteousness:
<br>
I will enter into them, I will give thanks unto تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">20</sup> This is the gate of تتراجراماتون;
<br>
The righteous shall enter into it.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">21</sup> I will give thanks unto thee; for thou hast answered me,
<br>
And art become my salvation.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">22</sup> The stone which the builders rejected
<br>
Is become the head of the corner [T.N. See Psalm 89:38 "rejected" same word].
<br>
<sup class="versenum">23</sup> This is تتراجراماتون’s doing;
<br>
It is marvellous in our eyes.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">24</sup> This is the day which تتراجراماتون hath made;
<br>
We will rejoice and be glad in it.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">25</sup> Save now, we beseech thee, O تتراجراماتون:
<br>
O تتراجراماتون, we beseech thee, send now prosperity.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">26</sup> Blessed be he that cometh in the name of تتراجراماتون:
<br>
We have blessed you out of the house of تتراجراماتون.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">27</sup> تتراجراماتون is Elokim, and he hath given us light:
<br>
Bind the sacrifice with cords, even unto the horns of the altar.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">28</sup> Thou art my Elokim, and I will give thanks unto thee:
<br>
Thou art my Elokim, I will exalt thee.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">29</sup> Oh give thanks unto تتراجراماتون; for he is good;
<br>
For his lovingkindness endureth for ever.
<br>
<br>
</div>
