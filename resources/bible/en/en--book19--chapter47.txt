<div class = "bible">
<sup class="versenum">1</sup>(For the Chief Musician. A Psalm of the sons of Korah) Oh clap your hands, all ye peoples;
<br>
Shout unto Elokim with the voice of triumph.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">2</sup> For تتراجراماتون Most High is terrible;
<br>
He is a great King over all the earth.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">3</sup> He subdueth peoples under us,
<br>
And nations under our feet.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">4</sup> He chooseth our inheritance for us,
<br>
The glory of Ya’qub (Jacob) whom he loved.
<br>
Selah
<br>
<sup class="versenum">5</sup> Elokim is gone up with a shout,
<br>
تتراجراماتون with the sound of a trumpet.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">6</sup> Sing praises to Elokim, sing praises:
<br>
Sing praises unto our King, sing praises.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">7</sup> For Elokim is the King of all the earth:
<br>
Sing ye praises with understanding.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">8</sup> Elokim reigneth over the nations:
<br>
Elokim sitteth upon his holy throne.
<br>
<sup class="versenum">9</sup> The princes of the peoples are gathered together
<br>
To be the people of the Elokim of Ibrahim (Abraham);
<br>
For the shields of the earth belong unto Elokim:
<br>
He is greatly exalted.
<br>
<br>
